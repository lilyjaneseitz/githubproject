How to create a webpage with GitHub

Step 1

Once you've created an account and are logged in, create a new repository.

Step 2

Name the repository, select "Public" and "Initialize...with a README", then click "Create repository".

Step 3

Click and drag your website folder to upload it, then click "Commit".

Step 4

Click "Settings".

Step 5

Scroll down to the "Source" section, click the drop-down menu and select "Master Branch".

Step 6

Your site URL will appear in the Source section.
